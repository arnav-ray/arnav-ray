My Learning Journey

This isn't just about coding. It's a full product development cycle undertaken by a non-technical person, driven by curiosity and a determination to build.

1

Ideation & Design Thinking

Every project began not with code, but with a problem. I applied design thinking principles to define user needs, sketch out UI/UX flows, and create a clear product vision before a single line of code was written.

2

AI-Powered Collaboration

I leveraged a suite of AI tools (Gemini, Claude, Cursor, ChatGPT) as my development partners. This went beyond simple code generation; I used them for architectural brainstorming, exploring different technical solutions, and translating my product vision into a tangible development plan.

3

Navigating the Technical Maze

The biggest challenge was overcoming the initial blockers. I wrestled with cryptic bugs, learned to debug systematically, and dove into concepts like API security and serverless architecture. Figuring out how to host a webpage securely was a project in itself, requiring persistence and a lot of trial and error.

4

Deployment & Iteration

Successfully deploying an application was a major milestone. The final step involved gathering user feedback, identifying areas for improvement, and iterating on the design and functionality‚Äîclosing the loop on a complete product development cycle.
